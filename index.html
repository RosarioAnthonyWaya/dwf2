Our story

DWF is dedicated to providing widowed families with the financial, emotional, and educational support they need to build a better future.

We provide resources such as skill acquisition programs and career plans for children, funding for businesses, covering basic necessities and tuition costs

How can you support us?



There are three main ways to support our mission at DWF. You can register your organization to train widows in practical skills, donate to help us provide resources and support, or volunteer your time and expertise.

By contributing in any of these ways, you help us empower widows with the tools, confidence, and opportunities they need to rebuild their lives. Your involvement makes a real difference in creating a future filled with dignity and hope.
